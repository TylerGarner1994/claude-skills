## AIDA

The foundational framework (Elias St. Elmo Lewis, 1898).

### Structure

**A - Attention:** Capture with compelling hook
**I - Interest:** Generate intellectual engagement through facts, stories, statistics
**D - Desire:** Create emotional want by transforming features into benefits
**A - Action:** Guide to conversion with clear, low-friction CTAs

### Supplement Example

**ATTENTION:**
> "WARNING: 97% of Adults Over 50 Are Deficient in This Critical Nutrient"

**ACTION:**
> "Click the button below to try MagComplete risk-free for 90 days. If you don't notice a dramatic difference in your sleep, energy, and overall wellbeing, we'll refund every pennyâno questions asked."

## PASTOR

Created by Ray Edwards. Shepherding framework that guides prospects.

### Structure

**P - Person/Problem/Pain:** Identify specific person and their pain
**A - Amplify:** Highlight consequences of not solving
**S - Story:** Share narrative of someone who overcame
**T - Transformation/Testimony:** Sell the transformation, not the method
**O - Offer:** Present product as the bridge
**R - Response:** Tell them exactly what to do next

## 4 U's Formula

Michael Masterson's framework for evaluating copy strength.

### Structure

Rate each element 1-4. If nothing scores 3+, revise.

**U - Useful:** How does this help the reader?
**U - Urgent:** Why act now?
**U - Unique:** What makes this different?
**U - Ultra-Specific:** Concrete numbers and details

### Headline Evaluation Example

**Weak headline:** "Try Our New Joint Supplement"
- Useful: 2 (vague benefit)
- Urgent: 1 (no urgency)
- Unique: 1 (nothing different)
- Ultra-Specific: 1 (no specifics)
- **Total: 5 â REWRITE**

**Strong headline:** "New UC-II Collagen Formula Reduces Joint Discomfort 40% in 90 DaysâOr Your Money Back (Introductory Pricing Ends Friday)"
- Useful: 4 (clear benefit)
- Urgent: 4 (deadline + intro pricing)
- Unique: 3 (specific ingredient UC-II)
- Ultra-Specific: 4 (40%, 90 days, Friday)
- **Total: 15 â STRONG**

## Before-After-Bridge

Focuses on aspirational transformation rather than amplifying pain.

### Structure

**Before:** Paint current struggle
**After:** Show desired future state
**Bridge:** Position product as path between them

### Supplement Example

**BEFORE:**
> "Right now, you're probably dealing with brain fog that makes afternoons feel like trudging through mud. You forget names, lose your train of thought mid-sentence, and need three cups of coffee just to feel half-awake."

> "Imagine waking up clear-headed. Sharp. Words come easily. You remember what you walked into the room for. Conversations flow without groping for vocabulary. You feel like yourself againâmaybe even better than you've felt in years."

> "NeuroClarity bridges that gap. It delivers the precise nutrients your brain has been starving forâin forms it can actually absorb and use. Within days, you'll notice the fog lifting. Within weeks, you'll wonder how you ever functioned without it."

## Feature-Advantage-Benefit

The FAB conversion chain for logical justification.

### Structure

**Feature:** What the product is or has
**Advantage:** Why it's better than alternatives
**Benefit:** What it means for the customer emotionally and practically

**Customers buy benefits, not featuresâbut the chain creates logical justification.**

### Supplement Example

| Feature | Advantage | Benefit |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| 500mg curcumin per capsule | 5x more than leading brands | Real relief, not just a "sprinkle" that does nothing |
| BioPerine black pepper extract | Increases absorption by 2,000% | Actually works because your body can use it |
| Enteric coating | Survives stomach acid intact | No upset stomach or "turmeric burps" |
| 90-day supply per bottle | 3x longer than competitors | Fewer reorders, better value, never run out |

## The Rule of One

Blockbuster copy focuses ruthlessly.

### Structure

**One Reader:** Write as if talking to a single ideal customer
**One Big Idea:** The central concept everything revolves around
**One Promise:** The primary outcome/benefit
**One Offer:** Clear, singular path forward

**The more points you cover, the weaker your message becomes.**

### Application

**Wrong approach:**
> "Our turmeric supports joint health, heart health, brain function, immune response, and healthy inflammation levels. It comes in capsules, powder, and liquid form at three different price points with multiple subscription options."

**Right approach:**
> "For the 50+ woman who refuses to let joint stiffness steal her active lifestyle: GoldenJoint's patented curcumin formula targets the root cause of joint discomfort so you can get back to the garden, the grandkids, and the life you love. One bottle. One simple capsule daily. One phone call to order."

| Situation | Recommended Framework |
|-----------|----------------------|

| Sales page, solution aware | PASTOR |
| Quick landing page | Before-After-Bridge |
| Headline testing | 4 U's evaluation |
| Complex offer, multiple benefits | AIDA with FAB chains |
| Sophisticated market, big launch | Rule of One + PASTOR |
